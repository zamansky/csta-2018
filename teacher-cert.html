NY State Certification Requirements

  • P12 license
  • 12 CS Credits in program
  • 50 fieldwork hours for certificate
  • Five major "areas":
    • Algorithms and Programming
    • Computing Systems
    • Data and Analysis
    • Impacts of computing
    • Networks and the Internet
